# DermIQ Phase 3 - Scanner, Analysis, Product Intelligence & Shelf Automation

## Objective
Build a unified product intelligence system connecting Scanner, Analysis, Shelf, Routine and Product Management.

## Unified Scanner
- Ingredient Scanner
- Barcode Scanner

Entry Points:
- Home Dashboard
- Scanner Tab
- Routine
- Shelf

- Ingredient Name
- Purpose
- Benefits
- Risk Level
- Suitability

Categories:
- Safe
- Use With Caution
- Potential Irritant

## Barcode Scanner
Fetch:
- Product Name
- Brand
- Image
- Category
- Ingredients

## Product Analysis
Display:
- Product Image
- Product Name
- Brand
- Category
- Overall Score

Statuses:
- Highly Recommended
- Recommended
- Neutral
- Not Recommended

## Save Product Flow
Actions:
- Save Product
- Add To Shelf
- Dismiss

## Shelf Integration

- Manual Add
- Scanner
- Shop Orders

## Shelf Management
Actions:
- View Product
- Edit Product
- Mark Empty
- Remove Product

## Expiry Tracking
Statuses:
- Safe
- Expiring Soon
- Expired

- Skin Type
- Hair Type
- Skin Concerns
- Hair Concerns

Results:
- Compatible
- Partially Compatible
- Not Compatible

Assign products to:
- AM Routine
- PM Routine

## Analysis History
Store:
- Scan Date
- Product
- Result

## Notifications

- Product Empty Alerts
- Routine Product Reminders
